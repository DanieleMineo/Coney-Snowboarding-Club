CONEY SNOWBOARDING CLUB

WE ARE BACK

We can think of no other phrase to describe our state of mind after a year of forced stop, after having seen one of the snowiest winters and not having been able to take full advantage of it! So the magical organization of CSC is ready to get back on the road to take you to discover or enjoy the magical world of snow. We will certainly be more flexible: we will quickly adapt to the safety standards that will be in force during every single outing to make you enjoy the skiable days in complete safety.
We are ready: IT'S TIME TO GO BACK TO LEAVE HOME !!

EVENTS

SURFING THE NET

To stay connected and develop the community, CSC surfs the net! The Classic Way: this site where you can find all the program and the telephone numbers of the organizers and above all pre-register. The Cool Way: join the “CSC” group on Facebook! Here you can get in direct contact with the organizers to ask for everything in your mind, or with the members who will confirm how you have fun with us.

OPENING TOUR

THE OPENING TOUR that sees us between September and November around the best clubs in the province of Treviso to export the desire to have fun and infect new future athletes, making known the spirit of CSC even away from the snow. If you have a club and want to organize a snow themed party, contact us! We will help you organize a memorable event! Follow us on Instagram and stay tuned for more updates

FREE STYLE SHOW

The show that you don't deserve but that one you need! On the 8th of December our best freestylers will fight on the magnificent SANPE snowpark with tricks of any type. There will be no lack of beer and adrenaline so the fun is absolutely GUARANTEED

CROSS SNOWBOARD CHAMPIONSHIPS

This year we are proud to say that we are part of the organization of the World Cross Snowboard Championships! The Championships will be in from 15 to 31 genuary and will see compete the best athletes from all over the world.

COURSES

BEGINNER

2 hours of lessons for 5 Sundays. Max 5 students per group. The dawn of your snowboard experience: the basic position, the first turns and the use of the ski lifts.

INTERMEDIATE

2 hours of lessons for 5 Sundays. Max 5 students per group. For those who already know how to go, a lot or a little, but want to go even more! Together we will improve the technique: conduct curve, serpentine and carving.

FREERIDE

2 hours of lessons for 5 Sundays. Max 5 students per group. Learning the absolute mastery of the board in all situations, descent techniques in powder snow, approach to jumps and technical maneuvers. Course reserved exclusively for riders of a good technical level. In case of low snow the course is managed like the Freestyle course. For this course, the use of a helmet is MANDATORY. Minimum age 16 years.

FREESTYLE

2 hours of lessons for 5 Sundays. Max 5 students per group. You'll learn how to go in the snow park in an easy and gradual way. Jump technique and rotating maneuvers, fakie gait. Course reserved exclusively for riders of a good technical level. For this course, the use of a helmet is MANDATORY. Minimum age 16 years.

SPLIT BOARD

4 full day course in the San Pellegrino area. The itineraries will be shared based on the experiences (and legs) of the participants.

COMPETITIVE FREESTYLE

10 full days with a qualified national coach. The course will be held on Sunday in the San Pellegrino area. Info, details and prices at info@csc.it.
